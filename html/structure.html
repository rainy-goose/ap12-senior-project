The Structure of the Internet

Section 2: Structure

IPv4

IPv4 is the most widely-used protocol as of 2018. However, the amount of available IPv4 addresses has been depleted since around 2015. Because of this, users are trying to get the most of IPv4 while it is still widely used, before IPv6 is transitioned in.

IPv6

IPv6 is the next-gen Internet Protocol, and the Internet has slowly beginning preparations to transition.
